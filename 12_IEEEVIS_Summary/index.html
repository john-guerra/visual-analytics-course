IEEEVis Summary

  • Main Visualization conference, along with CHI, and Eurovis
  • Organized by IEEE
  • About 1000+ attendees

Subconferences

  • Infovis -> Techniques
  • VAST -> The process
  • Scivis -> Rainbows
  • VizSec -> Security
  • VDS -> Big data
  • Workshops/Tutorials -> Special interests
  • Panels -> Debate
    • There was a special panel on how Scivis is dying

Observed themes

  • VegaLite
  • Network Visualization (Edge Bundling)
  • Text Visualization
  • Time Series
  • Machine Learning
  • Education, Design, Evaluation

VegaLite

*** Vega-Lite: A Grammar of Interactive Graphics (J) (Best Paper Award)

Authors: Arvind Satyanarayan, Dominik Moritz, Kanit Wongsuphasawat, and Jeffrey Heer

Next step after D3, define visualization from what you want rather than how to do it
